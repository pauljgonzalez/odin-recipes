Protien Oatmeal

Description

Upgrade your breakfast routine with our Protein-Packed Oatmeal recipe! Bursting with wholesome goodness and essential
nutrients, this delicious bowl of oatmeal is the perfect way to kick-start your day. With a generous serving of protein-rich
ingredients like Greek yogurt, nuts, and seeds, this oatmeal will keep you feeling full and satisfied until lunchtime. Plus, it's
customizable to suit your taste preferences, whether you prefer a sweet or savory twist. Say goodbye to boring breakfasts
and hello to a nourishing and energizing morning meal that's as tasty as it is nutritious!

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Protein Powder

Optional Ingredients

  • Fruits
  • Peanut Butter
  • Greek Yogurt

Steps

  1. Cook the oatmeal
  2. Stir in one scoop protein powder
  3. Add in an optional ingredients
  4. Enjoy!
